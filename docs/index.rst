How to Login Philo TV Account? | Sign In Now
====================

Introduction
============

Philo is a popular live TV streaming service focused on entertainment and lifestyle channels at an affordable price. To access your Philo account and start watching, you need to complete the login process. This article provides step‑by‑step instructions for logging into Philo TV on various devices.

Login Methods
=============

There are two primary ways to log in to Philo:

- **Direct login (email & password)** – Use your registered email and password.
- **TV code login** – For smart TVs, streaming players, and gaming consoles.

Web Browser Login
=================

Follow these steps to log in via a computer or mobile browser:

- Go to philo.com
- Click the **Log In** button at the top‑right corner.
- Enter the **email address** associated with your Philo account.
- Enter your **password**.
- Click **Log In** again.

.. note::
   If you have forgotten your password, click “Forgot password?” below the login fields. Philo will send a password reset link to your registered email.

Mobile App Login (iOS / Android)
================================

- Download the Philo app from the **Apple App Store** or **Google Play Store**.
- Open the app and tap **Log In**.
- Enter your **email** and **password**.
- Tap **Log In**.

Alternatively, use TV code login as described in the next section.

TV Device / Streaming Player Login
==================================

For devices such as **Roku, Apple TV, Amazon Fire TV, Android TV, or Chromecast**:

- Install and open the Philo channel / app on your device.
- You will see a **6‑character code** on your screen.
- On a separate computer or phone, go to philo.com/link.
- Log in with your email and password (if not already logged in).
- Enter the 6‑character code displayed on your TV.
- Click **Submit** or **Link Device**.

Your TV device should refresh automatically and log you in.

Troubleshooting Login Issues
============================

Common problems and solutions:

- **Issue: Incorrect email or password**
  Solution: Use “Forgot password” to reset. Ensure email is spelled correctly.

- **Issue: Cannot see the TV code**
  Solution: Restart the Philo app or your device. Check your internet connection.

- **Issue: “Too many devices” error**
  Solution: Philo allows 3 simultaneous streams. Go to Account Settings > Manage Devices to remove unused devices.

- **Issue: Login page not loading**
  Solution: Clear browser cache/cookies or try a different browser. For the app, reinstall or update to the latest version.

Managing Multiple Profiles
==========================

Philo allows up to 10 user profiles per account. After logging in:

- Click on your **profile icon** (top right on web, bottom navigation on mobile).
- Select **Add Profile** or **Manage Profiles**.
- Each profile has its own watch history and saved shows.

Logging Out
===========

To log out of Philo:

- **Web**: Click your profile icon → **Log Out**.
- **Mobile app**: Go to Settings → **Log Out**.
- **TV device**: Navigate to Settings → **Log Out** or simply unlink the device from your account via philo.com/link.

Security Tips
=============

- Always log out when using a shared or public computer.
- Do not share your password with others. Use profiles for family members.
- Enable two‑factor authentication (2FA) from Philo’s Account Security settings if available.
- Regularly review linked devices in your account settings.

Contact Support
===============

If you continue to experience login issues, reach out to Philo support:

- **Help Center**: support.philo.com
- **Email**: help@philo.com
- **Live chat**: Available on the Philo website during business hours.

Conclusion
==========

Logging into Philo TV is straightforward whether you use a web browser, mobile app, or TV device. Keep your login credentials secure, manage your active devices, and use the troubleshooting steps if needed. Once logged in, enjoy unlimited DVR and 60+ channels of entertainment.
